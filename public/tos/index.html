Description of Service

Anti-Bot is a Discord bot and web dashboard designed to protect Discord communities from automated spam accounts and scam bots. The service includes:

  • A Discord bot that monitors designated "quarantine" channels and takes automated actions against suspected bot accounts.
  • A web dashboard allowing server administrators to configure bot behavior, punishments, bypass lists, and log channels.
  • Authentication via Discord OAuth2 to verify server management permissions.

Anti-Bot is provided as-is, free of charge, and is an open-source project. We reserve the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any part of the service at any time without notice.

Account & Discord OAuth

Anti-Bot uses Discord's OAuth2 system for authentication. By logging in through the dashboard, you grant Anti-Bot permission to access:

  • Your Discord user identity (username, user ID, avatar).
  • The list of Discord guilds (servers) you are a member of, along with your permissions in each.

🔒 We only display servers where you hold the Manage Server or Administrator permission. We do not access your messages, email, or other personal data beyond what Discord provides.

Sessions are stored server-side and expire after 24 hours. You can log out at any time via the dashboard.

Bot Permissions & Automated Actions

When invited to a Discord server, Anti-Bot requires certain permissions to function. You, as the server administrator, are responsible for understanding and authorizing these permissions.

Anti-Bot may perform the following automated actions on members of your server based on your configuration:

  • Guild Ban — Permanently ban a user and delete their recent message history.
  • Guild Kick — Remove a user from the server without a ban.
  • Timeout — Temporarily restrict a user's ability to interact (up to 24 hours).
  • Message Deletion — Silently delete messages sent in monitored channels.

False positives may occur. Anti-Bot acts automatically based on channel activity. We are not liable for actions taken against legitimate human users. Always configure bypass roles for your moderators and trusted members.

By configuring and enabling Anti-Bot, you accept full responsibility for the automated actions it performs in your server.
